# UnderRun

Welcome to this terminal game , where you are portrayed as a shiobi who has to perform his duties and task as a responsible citizen and heir of the clan
The basic game idea revolves around the Clan of Fire, Water and other clans, which gives you buffs and XP , but the main talk is that, how fast and good are you at typing, cause the first round may be simple but the second round is brutal.
